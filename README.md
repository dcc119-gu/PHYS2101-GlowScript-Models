# PHYS2101-GlowScript-Models
Compilation of projects from computational modeling for PHYS2102: Electromagnetic Phenomena (August-December 2025). Descriptions of each lab are as follows: <br />
1. Introduction to basic while loops.  <br />
2. Used while loops to create spheres and arrows of set distances and radii.  <br />
3. Modeled a cart moving horizontally while launching a ball upwards and calculated time for ball to land back in cart.  <br />
4. Modeled vertical oscillation of mass on spring and calculated period of oscillation.  <br />
5. Modeled and calculated speed necessary for given spacecraft to maintain circular orbit around Earth.  <br />
6. Adapted code from lab 5 to model motion of craft dropped straight through center of Earth.  <br />
7. Adapted code from lab 6 to determine momentum of craft at center of Earth and potential energies at surface and center of Earth.  <br />
8. Modeled spacecraft crashing into moon given initial distance and velocity & calculated final speeds with energy principle.  <br />
9. Modeled ball bouncing in air and calculated time to reach maximum height with/without air resistance and estimated error of approximation.  <br />
10. Modeled nitrogen molecule given initial vibrational energy and calculated period of oscillation from resulting motion.  <br />
